Pickaxe Knockout was independently developed within the Fortnite Creative platform using Unreal Engine and the Verse scripting language. The game features a meticulously crafted environment including a custom-designed battle island and lobby area, with a core gameplay loop focused on the 30 unique power-ups focused on pickaxe combat. I led the project through all development stages, from initial design and asset creation to scripting, testing, marketing, deployment, and iteration on user feedback.
